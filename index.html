Welcome to Natural Resources Hub

Explore and Learn About Renewable and Non-Renewable Resources

Natural resources are materials from Earth that are used to support life and meet people's needs.
They include renewable resources like forests, water, and solar energy.
Non-renewable resources like fossil fuels and minerals are also essential.
❮ ❯

Types of Natural Resources

Renewable Resources

Resources that can be replenished naturally over time such as solar energy, wind, forests, and water.

Non-renewable Resources

Resources like fossil fuels and minerals that are limited and take millions of years to form.

How to Use Renewable Energies

Sustainable resource management ensures that the needs of the present are met without compromising future generations' ability to meet their needs. Renewable energy such as wind, solar, and hydropower is the key to sustainability.

  • Solar panels: Capture the sun's energy for electricity.
  • Wind turbines: Harness wind energy for power generation.
  • Hydropower: Use water flow to generate electricity.
